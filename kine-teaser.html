You show up every week.
So why does nobody build for you?

The options don't fit. Programmes built for men, beginner content you outgrow in weeks, or a sport that's more than most people are looking for. Hardly any intelligent training built around how women actually want to look, feel and progress.

02 — What Kine Actually Is

A programme built around you —
that teaches you as you follow it.

Most apps hand you a workout. Kine builds yours — around your experience, your goals, and your body. Then it explains every decision it made. Every session comes with context — not a textbook, just the right explanation at the right moment. After a few weeks, you stop Googling. After a few months, you don't need the explanation at all.

04 — What You Get

Everything a coach gives you.
Built into the programme.

Periodised programming

Structured blocks that build on each other. Load, volume, and intensity managed across weeks — not random workouts.

Exercise education

Every movement comes with context — what it targets, how to do it, and why it's in your session today.

Intelligent swaps

Can't do a movement? Swap it — and the programme explains what you're trading off so you're making an informed choice.

Cycle awareness

Optional period tracking built in. Another layer of context — not a rulebook. Works with your body, not against it.

Session analysis

After every session, feedback that sounds like a coach — what went well, what to watch, what comes next.

Weekly insights

A full summary after your last session. Total volume. Progressions. PRs. And a plain-English analysis of your week.

Programmed warmups

Targeted to your session. The warmup most people skip — because nobody programmes it for them.

Progress tracking

Progress measured by capability, not compliance. Miss a week? A welcome-back card shows everything you've built.
